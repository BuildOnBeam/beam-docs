# Beam App

We have a all-in-one application for managing all your Beam Wallet and Beam-related assets like currencies, NFTs, Bridging, Swapping and OnRamping using fiat currencies.

It's available at https://app.onbeam.com/ and https://app.testnet.onbeam.com/
