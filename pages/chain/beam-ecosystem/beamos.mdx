# BeamOS

BeamOS serves as your gateway into the Beam ecosystem. Access it at [os.onbeam.com](https://os.onbeam.com) to discover every tool, app, and feature the Beam ecosystem has to offer, all accessible with a single click.

With BeamOS, personalize your experience by setting your name, choosing from a variety of wallpapers, and defining your preferred app launcher settings.
