# Removing your collection

To remove your collection from Sphere, kindly submit a request through your dedicated Partner Success Manager or Business Development Lead. They will ensure a Sphere admin picks this up.

<Callout emoji="❗">
    **Important:** This action is irreversible. Please carefully consider your decision before requesting the permanent removal of your collection.
</Callout>
